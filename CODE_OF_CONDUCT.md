# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want GoMap to be a professional, respectful, and useful project for people learning, building, and testing security tooling.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to communicate with respect, assume good intent where reasonable, and keep discussions focused on improving the tool and its documentation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Give technical feedback clearly, without personal attacks.
- Keep examples and test cases limited to systems you own or are authorized to assess.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive information, credentials, tokens, or unauthorized scan results.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Encouraging unauthorized access, abuse, evasion against third-party systems, or harmful activity.
- Publishing secrets, private data, or exploit instructions aimed at real targets without authorization.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block users when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct concern, open a private security/contact channel where available, or contact the maintainer directly through the repository owner profile.
